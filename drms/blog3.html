ABOUT S. KING ?

Sirak Weldeab Habte (born February 17, 1996) Professionally known as S. KING is an Eritrean hip hop recording artist, record producer and sound engineer. His music was influenced by Dr.Dre, Kanye West, 2pac, Notorious B.I.G & Ice cube. KING started to rap at the age of 13 and a year later his interest and love in making music grew more and started to take it seriously. He started to perfect his production and engineering skills,However on January 25, 2020 he released his first EP (Art Of Vision), which was inspired by the book "The Principals And Power Of Vision.
