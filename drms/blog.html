WHO IS REZENÉ ?

REZENÉ is An up and surely emerging rap artist. Introduces Compilation of hard times, good times, self reflection, self awareness and the beauty of simplicity in everything. As the days go by he lives and thrives to write rap music that enables you to knock into your conscious mind and question everything and perhaps find an answer to them. His motive is to disable confinement and allow contentment to embark into you by making you feel a sense of engagement with his music. Learning and practicing are some of the key fundamentals to the trials in this life hence introducing a space where we all together can learn and inspire one another so since life is about taking chances take a chance, dig into yourself while listening to REZENÉ and watch yourself unfold beautiful vibrations through his music.

ABOUT May i?

This project is made to inspire the listener. It comes from a catastrophic place to a light so bright it shows you a way of his truth, REZENÉ. with its harsh approach it takes to spark something positive inside of you. Art is made to provoke. With the controversial track like ‘?’ Rez helps us focus on the questions in order to reinsure our stance while records like ‘Runnin’, Since & 17’ speak about a dark depression state, a common theme which everyday people go through. ‘Been’ with it’s harsh lyrics and playful style talks about race, degrading, classism and other handful things that go hand in hand when talking about hate. ending with a message from the late great Tupac Shakur. ‘May i?’ is not only my first but one that i recommend you to listen too, helps you identify with yourself knowing somebody out there is speaking of what your going through, Understand REZENÉ as a person and a artist.
