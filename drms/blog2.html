WHO IS NEGUSWUN ?

Abel (Neguswun) was 12, His single mother would help him sneak his way in to the choirs rehearsal room every saturday night despite the fact that she was one of the strong Christian religious women believer. From then He’d start learning how to play chords, melodies but mostly the biggest lesson I would say he learned over the time spent with choirs was how to adapt to a certain types of music and melodies in a short amount time, and He’d would start playing the same melody/chords again and again for weeks till developed what I would like to call “the mechanism of adapting and manipulating sounds” to certain levels of music and genre.
